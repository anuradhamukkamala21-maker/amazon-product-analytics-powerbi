# Amazon Product Analytics Dashboard

Power BI dashboard analyzing Amazon product data to uncover insights on product categories, customer ratings, discounts, reviews, and pricing trends.

## Overview

This project transforms Amazon product data into an interactive dashboard that helps understand:

- Product distribution across categories
- Customer rating patterns
- Discount trends
- Most reviewed products
- Relationship between product price and customer ratings

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Dashboard

Key metrics including:

- Total Products
- Average Rating
- Average Discount
- Total Reviews

---

### Category & Product Insights

Analysis of:

- Products by Category
- Average Rating by Category
- Average Discount by Category
- Top 10 Most Reviewed Products

---

### Category Distribution Analysis

Visual representation of category concentration using:

- Donut Chart
- Treemap
- Category Filter

---

### Pricing & Rating Analysis

Scatter plot showing the relationship between:

- Product Price
- Customer Rating
- Review Volume
- Product Category

## Key Insights

- Electronics is the largest product category.
- Average customer rating is 4.1/5.
- Average product discount is 47.7%.
- Products have accumulated more than 27 million customer reviews.
- Product price shows limited correlation with customer ratings.
